# RAS: Robotics & Autonomous Systems

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for **RAS**, a specialized research and development hub located at the **German Jordanian University (GJU)**.

---

## Our Mission
The RAS Lab is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of **Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)** and **Autonomous Systems**. We focus on transforming complex theoretical concepts into functional, real-world robotic solutions.

Our laboratory serves as a sandbox for innovation in:
* **Autonomous Flight:** Development of self-navigating drones using SLAM and computer vision.
* **Intelligent Control:** Implementing advanced flight algorithms for stability and precision.
* **System Integration:** Bridging the gap between hardware (sensors/actuators) and high-level AI.
